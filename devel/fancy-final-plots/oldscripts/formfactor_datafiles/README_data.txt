mu*Gep/Gmp
==============================================================
--------------------------------------------------------------
FFR_Gayou.dat

   ref: O. Gayou et al., Phys. Rev. Lett. 88, 092301 (2002)
--------------------------------------------------------------
FFR_Gayou2.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  mu*Gep/Gmp  error(stat)  error(syst)
   ref: O. Gayou et al., Phys. Rev. C 64, 038202 (2001)
--------------------------------------------------------------
FFR_Jones.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  mu*Gep/Gmp  error(stat)  error(syst)
   ref: M.K. Jones et al., PRL vol. 84, 1398 (2000)
--------------------------------------------------------------
FFR_Punjabi.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  mu*Gep/Gmp  error(stat)  error(syst)
   ref: Punjabi (???)
--------------------------------------------------------------
FFR_Pospischil.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  mu*Gep/Gmp  error(stat)  error(syst)
   ref: Pospischil (???)
--------------------------------------------------------------
FFR_Dieterich.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  mu*Gep/Gmp  error(stat)  error(syst)
   ref: S. Dieterich et al., Phys. Lett. B 500, 47 (2001)
  note: (in the paper it's given as Ge/Gm, but Gm is normalized
        to mu already (Gm(0) = 1 in the paper).)
--------------------------------------------------------------
FFR_Milbrath_p.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  mu*Gep/Gmp  error(stat)  error(syst)
   ref: B.D. Milbrath et al., Phys. Rev. Lett. 82, 2221 (1999)
  note: from ep->ep, this is the erratum of another paper:
        B.D. Milbrath et al., Phys. Rev. Lett. 80, 452 (1998)
--------------------------------------------------------------
FFR_Milbrath_d.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  mu*Gep/Gmp  error(stat)  error(syst)
   ref: B.D. Milbrath et al., Phys. Rev. Lett. 82, 2221 (1999)
  note: from ed->ep, this is the erratum of another paper:
        B.D. Milbrath et al., Phys. Rev. Lett. 80, 452 (1998)
--------------------------------------------------------------
FFR_Andivahis.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  mu*Gep/Gmp  error(+stat)  error(-stat)  error(+total)  error(-total)
   ref: L. Andivahis et al., Phys. Rev. D 50, 5491 (1994)
--------------------------------------------------------------
FFR_Bartel.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  mu*Gep/Gmp  error(total)
   ref: W. Bartel et al    Nuclear Physics B58    429   (1973)
--------------------------------------------------------------
FFR_Litt.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  mu*Gep/Gmp  error(total)
   ref: J. Litt et al., Phys. Lett. 31B, 40, (1970)
--------------------------------------------------------------
FFR_Berger.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  mu*Gep/Gmp  error(stat)
   ref: Ch. Berger et al., Phys Letters 35B, 87-89,(1971)
  note: "The shown errors are random errors only. In case of Ge and Gm, a normalization 
        error of 2% has to be added."  
        note that in paper Q2 is in (1/fm)^2

GEp_Walker.dat

--------------------------------------------------------------
GEp_Borokowski.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  Gep/Gd  error(stat)  Gep  error(stat)
   ref: F.Borkowski et al., Nucl Phys B93, 461-478,(1975)
  note: see paper for note on errors.
--------------------------------------------------------------
GEp_Simon.dat
format: Q2(fm-2)  GEp  GEp/Gd   error(stat, in %) 
   ref: G.G. Simon et al., Nucl. Phys. A, 381-391 (1979)
  note:
--------------------------------------------------------------
GEp_Murphy.dat
format: Q2(fm-2)  Q2(GeV/c^2)  GEp/Gd   error(total)  GEp   error(total)  
   ref: J.J.Murphy et al., Phys Rev C9, 2125-2129 (1974)
  note: Q2 in (GeV/c)^2, GEp/Gd (and its errors) were not in the paper.
        (calculated for the database.)
--------------------------------------------------------------
GEp_Bartel.dat
format: Q^2 (GeV/c)^2  Gep/Gd  error(total)
   ref: W. Bartel et al    Nuclear Physics B58    429   (1973)
  note: GEp extracted from (mu*GEp/GMp) and GMp, see database for
        notes on errors.
--------------------------------------------------------------
GEp_Hanson.dat
format: Q2(fm-2)  Q2(Gev^2)  GEp/Gd  error(total)  GEp  error(total,relative in %) 
   ref: K.M.Hanson(Harvard) et al., Phys Rev D8    753-778   (1973)
  note: careful with the errors on GEp!  the number is the relative error IN PERCENT.
        (the error on GEp/Gd is the absolute error.)
--------------------------------------------------------------
GEp_Price.dat
format:  Q2(GeV^2)    GEp/Gd   error(total)   GEp^2   error(total)    
   ref: L.E.Price(Harvard University) et al., Phys Rev D4, 45-53,(1971)
  note: GEp/Gd extracted from numbers in paper.    
--------------------------------------------------------------
GEp_Berger.dat
format: Q2(GeV^2)   GEp/Gd   error(stat)
   ref: Ch. Berger et al., Phys Letters 35B, 87-89,(1971)
  note: numbers are calculated from numbers in paper.
--------------------------------------------------------------
GEp_Hand.dat
L.N. Hand et al., Rev. Mod. Phys., 35, 335-349 (1963)
format: Q2(GeV^2)   GEp   error(---)
   ref: L.N. Hand et al., Rev. Mod. Phys., 35, 335-349 (1963)
  note: this paper gives data for several experiments, please 
        see paper for details.
